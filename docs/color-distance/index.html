Key finding

No perceptual metric beats sRGB for this sparse palette. All OkLab variants map chromatic colors to achromatic entries (light-gray) too readily. sRGB's equal-weight RGB channels act as implicit hue preservation. Even CIEDE2000 (the CIE gold standard) maps skin tone to gray.
